Why we build

We’re four Christians who build software because we believe small, useful tools are a quiet way to serve people. The apps we make are the apps we want to use ourselves — and the kind we’d hand to our families without a disclaimer.

That means: no dark patterns, no engagement traps, no ads that follow you home. It also means we take our time. We’d rather ship something small that works than something big that wastes your attention.

Our faith shapes how we work, but it isn’t the price of admission. Whoever you are, we hope these apps are useful to you.
